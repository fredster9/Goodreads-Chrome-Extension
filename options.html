This app works on two types of pages:
  1. Amazon ebook pages (example)
  2. The Goodreads "Want To Read" shelf page (example)
  3. The Goodreads book page (example)

Here's what you can do on each page:

Amazon ebook pages

  1. See if you've rated the book on Goodreads
  2. If you haven't
    • See if you've rated other books by the author
    • Check your library for availability
    • Open it on library or Goodreads

Goodreads 'Want To Read' shelf pages

  • Automatically check your library for ebook and audiobook availability
  • Open titles on library website

Goodreads book page

  • Automatically check to see if you've rated other books by this author
